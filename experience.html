My Experience. Since day one.

Headboy of Maktab Sabah

Starting as a regular school prefect in Form 1, I was later appointed as Junior Headboy in Form 2, Vice Headboy (ii) in Form 3, Vice Headboy (i) in Form 4 and was honored to become the Headboy of Maktab Sabah and the Pengerusi of Majlis Pemimpin Pelajar at Maktab Sabah in 2021 (Form 5).

#MaktabSabahPrihatin

I was appointed as the Co-Program Director for the Maktab Sabah Prihatin Program, organized by myself and the YDP MPP. This program was entirely initiated of us the students to assist those who affected by the Covid-19 pandemic. Special thanks to Yang Berbahagia Datin Seri Pangilma Datuk Dr. Hajah Nuinda binti Hj. Alias (Ex-Principal) for the full support through the event.

Anugerah Perdana

I was honored with the PIBG Excellence Service Award and the Service Recognition Award during the Majlis Anugerah Kecemerlangan Maktab Sabah, held about 3-4 months after the SPM. These awards hold great significance for me, symbolizing the highlight of my five-year journey in secondary school.

#InnovateTheFuture

I served as the Program Director for the HPD Community Service program, which focused on Science, Technology, and Mathematics (STEM) at SK Kampung Empila, Kota Samarahan. It was my first time leading a program within the university and Alhamdulillah, it was successfully completed and thanks to the hard work and dedication of my committee members.

INFORMS Sports Day

I took the lead in organizing the INFORMS Sports Day under the organization of the INFORMS Society at UiTM Sarawak, Samarahan Campus. The program featured a Men's Futsal competition and a Women's Netball competition, with the participation of nearly 80 students from the School of Information Science.

INFORMS eSports Day

I also took the lead in organizing the INFORMS eSports Day and opened participation to all faculties at the Samarahan Campus to create a more vibrant program atmosphere. The aim of this event was to empower the society in delivering quality programs in line with the expectations of HEP.

INFORMS Gawai Raya

Furthermore, I took the lead in organizing the INFORMS Gawai Raya program, which was the theme for the PPSM Appreciation and Dean's Award Ceremony held at Ruai Pengawa, UiTM Samarahan Campus. This program was specifically designed to honor students who achieved the Dean's Award, while fostering a closer, more familial atmosphere within the faculty.

INFORMS Digital Innovation Colloquium

As part of the effort to build students' confidence to the highest level, I, along with the society advisor, agreed to organize the INFORMS Digital Innovation Colloquium as a platform for presenting projects from specific subjects. We also offered a cash prize as the main reward.

INFORMS General Meeting 2024

Recently, I organized the INFORMS General Meeting which saw the involvement of new students in their first semester. This program is a key event every semester to introduce new students to the academic environment and culture of the School of Information Science.The program was a success. Thanks to the INFORMS family.

Intership IPK PDRM

My diploma internship in Information Management provided me with practical experience in handling data, managing records, and understanding workplace operations. It was an invaluable opportunity to apply my knowledge, develop new skills, and gain insight into the professional world of information management.
